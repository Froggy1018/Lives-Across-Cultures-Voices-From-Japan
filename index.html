About Dr. Saga
Dr. Junichi Saga is a Japanese physician and writer from Tsuchiura, Ibaraki Prefecture, renowned for documenting the oral histories of his elderly patients. While practicing medicine in rural Japan, he began recording their life stories, recognizing their value in preserving the country's vanishing traditions and social memory. His works, including Confessions of a Yakuza and Memories of Silk and Straw, offer rare insights into Japan's cultural and moral landscape in the early 20th century. Through his writing, Dr. Saga bridges generations, giving voice to ordinary people whose stories illuminate the human side of Japan's history.
